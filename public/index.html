What is ShillShare?

SkillShare is a collaboration tool that connects you to developers with the skills you need for any given project. Simply enter the technical skills you require and ShillShare finds you people the right people for the job.

Advanced Algorithms

ShillShare uses the most advanced algorithms taught in CMPE130 to provide you with the most optimal results. This website implements hashing, sorting, and graph theory.
